🌟 Overview

Smart Bharat AI is an AI-powered digital governance platform designed to simplify the way citizens interact with government services.

Inspired by the vision of Digital India and modern government platforms like UMANG, Smart Bharat AI leverages Generative AI to provide a personalized, secure, multilingual, and intelligent experience for accessing government schemes, reporting civic issues, verifying documents, and navigating public services.

Unlike traditional government portals, Smart Bharat AI acts as an AI Companion, guiding citizens through every step—from discovering schemes to applying online using verified official government links.

🎯 Problem Statement

Citizens often face challenges such as:

Finding the correct government website
Understanding complex government documents
Identifying eligible schemes
Filling application forms correctly
Tracking complaints
Navigating multiple government portals
Language barriers
Lack of personalized assistance

Smart Bharat AI addresses these challenges through AI-driven assistance, official government resources, and an intuitive user experience.
